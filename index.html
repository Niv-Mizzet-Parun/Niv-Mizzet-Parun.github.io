About

I turn complex product ideas into stories people remember and act on.

I steer brand narrative from brainstorm to final deliverable — shaping multi-channel content calendars, building messaging frameworks, and translating dense technical concepts into language that users actually trust. I partner across social, product, sales, and developer teams to connect data insights to creative direction so every campaign launches on time and hits the mark.

I thrive where technical depth meets creative narrative. Before content, I spent six years running the coaching floor and media operation at a gym — which taught me that behavior change is just good storytelling with a deadline. That same instinct drives everything I do in marketing.

Always learning, always iterating, and always focused on stories that move business forward.
